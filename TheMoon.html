The Moon

The moon is an astronomical body that orbits our planet, Earth

A Little Introduction on the Moon

Without our moon, life on Earth would be drastically different. The moon moderates the Earth's wobble on its axis. This helps give us a stable climate, in comparison to some other planets. Scientists believe the moon was likely formed after a collision between Earth and another celestial body.
